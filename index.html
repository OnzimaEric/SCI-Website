About SCI

Safe Communities International (SCI) is a non-profit, community-focused organization established to promote safety, health, security, and resilience among communities and institutions. Headquartered in Uganda with an international outlook, SCI aligns its work with WHO guidance, the UN SDGs, and global best practices in safety and risk management.

Our Mission

To promote safety, health, security, and resilience through evidence-based interventions and partnerships.

Our Objectives

  • Reducing preventable injuries, occupational hazards, and safety risks.
  • Strengthening emergency preparedness and response capacity.
  • Supporting sustainable safety systems in communities and workplaces.

Our Services

Occupational H&S

Risk assessments, audits, investigations.

Community Safety

Public safety campaigns and training.

Emergency Preparedness

DRR, fire safety, first aid.

Training & Advisory

Capacity building and compliance support.
