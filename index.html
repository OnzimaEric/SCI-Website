Company Overview

Safe Communities International (SCI) is a leading organization dedicated to advancing safety, health, security, and community resilience worldwide. We partner with institutions, industries, and communities to identify risks, strengthen capacity, and implement practical, evidence-based solutions that protect people, property, and the environment.

Guided by international best practices and local insights, SCI delivers safety solutions that transcend geographical, cultural, and organizational boundaries. Our programs focus on preventing accidents, enhancing emergency preparedness, and fostering sustainable, resilient communities.

Through strategic collaborations with organizations and stakeholders, SCI continues to scale its impact, drive innovation in safety solutions, and strengthen communities globally.

Founders’ Message

At Safe Communities International (SCI), our work is driven by a shared conviction: Safety is not optional; it is foundational to sustainable development and resilient societies.

Anthony Onzima

Co-Founder & Director – Risk, Safety and Resilience (Lead Risk Analyst & Strategic Safety Advisor)

“Across industries and communities, many losses are preventable. At SCI, we focus on understanding risk before it becomes harm using data, standards, and practical experience to design solutions that protect lives, assets, and the environment. Our goal is to help organizations and communities move from reactive response to proactive prevention.”

Eric Erutu

Co-Founder & Director – Training, Capacity Building and Competence Development (Senior Training & Workforce Development Lead)

“Sustainable safety is achieved when people are empowered with the right knowledge, skills, and mindset. At SCI, we believe training is not just about compliance; it is about competence, confidence, and culture. When individuals are well trained, safety becomes part of everyday decision-making.”

Together, the SCI leadership team brings technical expertise, field experience, and a deep understanding of local and international safety challenges. This combination enables SCI to deliver solutions that are practical, credible, and aligned with global best practices while remaining grounded in real-world conditions.
